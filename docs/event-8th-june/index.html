Inaugural Meeting

In June 2022 we held our inaugural meeting at the University of Birmingham, with attendees from several cities across the Midlands. This event was organised by a committee of volunteers, and chaired by Gavin Yearwood.

Our organiser committee for this year are James Carpenter, Andrew Edmondson, Adrian Garcia, James Tripp, Heather Turner, Godwin Yeboah, and Jenny Wong.

10:00 - 10:10 - RSE Midlands Welcome

11:30 – 12:00 - Talk 2 – Rachael Ainsworth (SSI), “The Software Sustainability Institute Community and Events”

15:00 - 15:15 - Refreshment break

15:15 - 15:30 - Talk 6 – Matt Williams (Society of Research Software Engineering)

15:30 - 16:00 - Closing Talk
